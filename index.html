Frequently Asked Questions

How is life expectancy calculated? +
We use WHO life expectancy data for your country and gender as a baseline, then adjust based on health factors like BMI, smoking status, exercise frequency, and alcohol consumption.
Is this meant to be depressing? +
Not at all! The goal is to inspire intentional living. By visualizing your time, you're motivated to focus on what truly matters and make meaningful progress on your life goals.
Is my data private? +
Yes. Your personal data is stored securely and is only accessible to you. We never share or sell your information.
Is it free to use? +
Yes! MyLife is completely free to use. Sign in with Google and start tracking your life goals today.
